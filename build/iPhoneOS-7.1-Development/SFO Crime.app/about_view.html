Disclaimer:

This application is not affiliated with the City of San Francisco or the San Francisco Police Department.

We use publicly available information to generate the map and pinpoints of reported crimes. Any errors, omissions, incorrect point placement, etc. are a result of the reported data. Sensitive locations (such as sexual assault, etc.) are not mapped. We limit the mappings to 1,000 incidents so be mindful when selecting large date ranges.

As of September 2015, the San Francisco Police Department delays the release of crime data by 2 weeks. Any date range selections within the past 2 weeks will likely return an empty result.

Enhancements to Data:

Minor adjustments may be made to the pin locations when there is a lot of activity in one location. This is a convenience to you so that the pinpoints are not stacked on top of each other on the map.
